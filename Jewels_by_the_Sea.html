    Jewels by the Sea

    UX DESIGN / RESEARCH / BRANDING

    In today's day and age where a heavy emphasis is placed on technology, a good website is crucial for the success of a business. Websites, more often than not, are ways of staying connected to a business and may even be someone's first impression of the business. A good website with strong branding can build trust with the customer while simultaneously forming a connection. Good websites, especially for small business with little resources, are hard to come by. Thus, we decided to reach out to a small business with a sole owner, Linda Jalving, to redesign her website and online identity.

    Initial Requirements Gathering

    We visited Jewels by the Sea in person and asked a series of questions in order to understand what is expected from us. We asked questions relating to website content & design, target demographics, her business model, and did an evaluation of the current site. From this, we created a priority list to map out what we can and should get done in our 10 week time frame.

    User Interviews

    After gaining an understanding of Jewels by the Sea's expectations, we moved on to interviewing people.

    We wanted to target regulars of Jewels by the Sea as well as potential shoppers who may want to stop by. Thus, we had Linda provide us with contact information of some of her regulars and interviewed pedestrians around the shop.     From our insights, we came up with 3 user personas: the regular gift shopper, tourist, and potential shopper.
